TrustTrade Platform Overview

TrustTrade is a business asset marketplace for buying and selling commercial assets, surplus inventory, industrial equipment, office equipment, and related B2B items.

Core idea
- It is not a retail shopping site.
- Listings are tied to business identity and platform workflows.
- Users move from discovery to interest, negotiation, quote, reservation, and payment.

Main roles
- Guest: browse public marketplace and public business pages.
- Buyer: compare assets, send interest, negotiate, and pay.
- Seller: create businesses, post assets, manage leads, and review analytics.
- Admin: supervise users, businesses, orders, and support.

Core workflows
1. Register or sign in.
2. Create or choose a business.
3. Post assets under that business.
4. Browse the marketplace and inspect listings.
5. Use the dashboard to manage leads, listings, and analytics.
6. Move accepted deals toward quote, reservation, checkout, and completion.
