Business Management

Purpose
Business management is the seller workflow for creating and maintaining a business identity on TrustTrade.

Main routes
- /my-businesses
- /my-businesses/new
- /my-businesses/:id
- /businessdetails/:businessId
- /businessdetails/:businessId/listings

Backend routes
- GET /api/businesses
- POST /api/businesses
- PUT /api/businesses/:id
- DELETE /api/businesses/:id
- GET /api/businesses/:id
- GET /api/businesses/:id/assets

Business fields in the data model
- owner
- businessName
- images
- location.city
- location.place
- description

What sellers can do
- list all their businesses
- create a new business
- edit a business
- delete a business
- inspect public business pages
- attach assets to a business

Why it matters
- TrustTrade listings are tied to businesses, not just user accounts.
- Business context improves trust and makes listings feel real.

Useful AI keywords
- manage business
- create business
- business profile
- public business page
- business assets
