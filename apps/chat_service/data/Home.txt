Home Page

Purpose
The Home page is the signed-in overview screen after login. It combines discovery, promotions, and quick next actions.

Main route
- /home

Who uses it
- buyers
- sellers
- admins with authenticated access

What it shows
- featured event or campaign content
- marketplace highlights
- trending assets
- new arrivals
- role-aware action buttons

How it works
- Loads home stats from the backend.
- Loads featured event data.
- Uses those responses to render the hero section and discovery cards.

Why it matters
- It is the bridge between login and deeper workflows.
- It is not the full seller dashboard.
- It is not the checkout page.
- It is not the main lead-management workspace.

Useful AI keywords
- signed-in overview
- featured event
- marketplace highlights
- post login landing
- dashboard shortcut
