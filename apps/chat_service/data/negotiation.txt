Negotiation

Purpose
Negotiation is the TrustTrade workflow for buyers and sellers to discuss price, quantity, and deal terms before payment.

Where it starts
- Asset details page
- User chooses either Express Interest or Begin Negotiation

What the buyer can do
- write a message describing the offer or negotiation goal
- mention quantity if relevant
- submit the interest as a negotiating request

What the backend stores
- assetId
- buyer message
- quantity
- status = negotiating
- negotiationStartDate when the status changes into negotiation

What the seller can do
- review the lead in the seller dashboard
- accept the negotiation
- reject the negotiation
- keep negotiating through the lead workflow

How the AI agent should explain it
- Do not invent buttons that do not exist in the app.
- Do not mention Message Seller or Make an Offer unless the UI actually exposes them.
- Use the real TrustTrade language: Express Interest, Begin Negotiation, negotiating lead, accepted, rejected, quote, reservation, and checkout.

Agent purchase mode negotiation
- In agent mode, the purchase graph can record negotiation words through the backend negotiation API.
- The negotiation step is part of the structured buying flow before quote, reservation, and payment.

Useful AI keywords
- express interest
- begin negotiation
- negotiating lead
- accepted offer
- rejected offer
- negotiation start date
